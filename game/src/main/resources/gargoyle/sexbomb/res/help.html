You must locate all mines (or "bombs") in a mine field as quickly as possible. You do this by uncovering squares that do not contain a mine using your left mouse button, and marking squares that do with a flag, using your right mouse button.

When you uncover a square, you will either see a mine (and lose thegame), or the number of mines directly adjacent to (or "touching") thatsquare, ranging from 1 through 8. (If there are no mines touching the square, you automatically uncover all adjacent empty squares.)

The objective of the game is to reach a certain point score(depending on the skill level you have chosen, see below) as fast aspossible.
